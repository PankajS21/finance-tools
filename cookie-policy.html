Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to enhance your experience while protecting your privacy

Last Updated: January 2024 | Version: 2.0

Cookie Notice

FinanceTools uses minimal cookies to improve functionality. We respect your privacy and only use essential cookies by default. Optional cookies require your explicit consent.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

First-Party Cookies

Set by FinanceTools directly to enable core functionality and remember your preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services we use (like analytics) with your explicit consent only.

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies

Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until deleted.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year
session_id Maintains your session for security Session
calculator_settings Saves your calculator preferences locally 30 days
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery Session

2.2 Performance Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttle request rate 1 minute

2.3 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme_preference Remembers your theme preference (light/dark) 1 year
language_preference Remembers your language preference 1 year

3. How to Manage Cookies

3.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and security ‚Üí Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions ‚Üí Cookies and site data

3.2 On Our Website

When you first visit FinanceTools, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

Important Note

If you choose to disable essential cookies, some features of our website may not work properly, such as calculator settings persistence and session management.

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Analytics Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website. This service uses cookies to collect anonymous information such as:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device and browser information

4.2 Social Media Cookies

We do not use social media plugins that set cookies. Any sharing buttons use privacy-friendly implementations that don't track you.

5. Data Protection

5.1 Anonymized Data

All optional cookies collect anonymized data that cannot be used to identify you personally.

5.2 No Cross-Site Tracking

We do not participate in cross-site tracking or retargeting advertising networks.

5.3 Data Retention

Cookie data is retained only for as long as necessary for its intended purpose, as specified in the duration column of our cookie tables.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through our website.

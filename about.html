Our Story

Started in 2020

Born from a simple idea: Make financial planning accessible to everyone

FinanceTools was founded in 2020 with a clear mission: to democratize financial planning by providing free, easy-to-use calculators that were previously only available through paid financial advisors or complex spreadsheets.

We noticed that people were making important financial decisions without proper tools to analyze their options. Many were paying high fees for simple calculations or using outdated methods.

Our team of financial experts and developers came together to create a platform that would provide accurate, up-to-date financial calculators completely free of charge. We believe that everyone deserves access to tools that can help them make better financial decisions.

Today, we serve over 50,000 users monthly across 100+ countries, helping people plan their loans, investments, taxes, and retirement with confidence.

Our Mission

To make financial literacy accessible through free, accurate, and easy-to-use tools.

We're committed to breaking down the barriers to financial planning by providing:

  • Free access to professional-grade financial calculators
  • Educational resources to improve financial literacy
  • Privacy-focused tools that respect user data
  • Mobile-optimized tools for on-the-go planning
